You can invite friends and family around you to use PathMemo. When a friend registers and tries it out through your invitation, both of you will receive Pro membership rewards.

How to invite:

1. Go to "Personal Center" → "My Invitations"
2. Tap "Forward to Friends" to send a mini-program card, or tap "Share to Moments" to generate a QR code image — save it and share to your Moments

![Invite rewards](/tutorial7-1.webp)

Invitation reward rules:
- When a friend registers through your invitation, they immediately receive 7 days of Pro membership
- As the inviter, you also receive 7 days of Pro membership for each successful invitation
- You can earn up to 14 days of Pro membership per month through invitations (valid permanently)

View invitation history:
On the "My Invitations" page, you can see the list of friends you've invited. Contact customer service if you have any issues.
