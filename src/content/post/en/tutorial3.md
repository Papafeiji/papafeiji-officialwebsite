You can save your daily itinerary as an image and share it with family or friends.

1. Enter a specific day's diary
2. Tap the "Share" button at the top-right corner

[![Share](https://papafeiji.cn/tutorial3-1.png)](weixin://dl/business/?appid=wx179ae36324c7c502&path=pages/index/index&env_version=release)

PS. This feature can also download diary images to your local device or share to Moments.
PS. When sharing your itinerary with friends, we add a small footer to the card — thank you for recommending PathMemo to more users!
