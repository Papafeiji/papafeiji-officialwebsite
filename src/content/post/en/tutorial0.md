By following the [PathMemo] WeChat service account, you can ask questions directly through the service account for easier access.

![WeChat pinned chat settings](https://papafeiji.cn/tutorial0-1.jpg)

Steps:
1. Follow the [PathMemo] service account
2. Follow the prompts to link your mini-program account
3. Open the service account to ask questions directly; conversation history is saved permanently
4. We recommend pinning the PathMemo service account for quicker access

![WeChat pinned chat settings](https://papafeiji.cn/tutorial0-2.png)
